Wynncraft Original Character series

Original characters designed and created on the given world structure and storyline of the game, "Wynncraft".
The 5 characters each represent a class in the game, including Warrior, Mage, Archer, Assassin and Shaman.
The four seasons were designed as the four festivals (Blizzard, heroes, Bonfire, Spirits) as an in-game event.
